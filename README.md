## Introduction
Echoes of Civilization is a Vanilla+ mod that aims to enhance the core gameplay experience of Hytale. Explore undead ruins to uncover ancient human writings that contain knowledge of lost technology. 
The mod adds 6 new crafting recipes and 4 new items that are designed for quality of life, seamless integration with existing items and mechanics, and fun.


## Mod Features
- New Items/Recipes:
  - **Musket**: A powerful firearm that fires a single projectile that deals high damage. Slow to reload. Reworked from the existing Blunderbuss item.
  - **(NEW)** **Musket Shot**: Ammunition for the Musket. Crafted from iron, cloth, and boompowder.
  - **Glider**: A lightweight glider that allows players to soar through the skies.
  - **(NEW)** **Plow**: A farming tool that tills soil in a 3x3 area.
  - **(NEW)** **Tree Saw**: A specialized saw for quickly cutting down large trees. Cuts logs in a 3x3 area.
  - **(NEW)** **Timepiece**: An intricate mechanical device that displays the current date and time.
